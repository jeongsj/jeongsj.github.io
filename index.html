About Me

Welcome. I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Sociology at Chung-Ang University, South Korea.

My research interests lie at the intersection of social movements, gender, and sexuality. Currently, I am conducting research on the history of the South Korean LGBTQ rights movement and the dynamics of anti-discrimination politics. I am also involved in quantitative research projects examining health disparities among sexual minorities in Korea.

I aim to contribute to a better sociological understanding of how marginalized groups mobilize for their rights and how social institutions affect their well-being.
