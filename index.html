About Me

Welcome. I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Sociology at Chung-Ang University, South Korea.

My research interests lie at the intersection of social movements, gender, and sexuality. currently, I am conducting research on the history of the South Korean LGBTQ movement. I am also involved in quantitative research projects examining health disparities among sexual and gender minorities in Korea.

I aim to contribute to a better sociological understanding of how marginalized groups mobilize for their rights and how social institutions affect their well-being.
